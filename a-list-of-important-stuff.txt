`ProgramOneFirstEstelScript` https://github.com/segmentar/program-one-first-estel-script

	This solution swash project syntactically parses a form from a text file onto a raw binary file .

	(.) <header::fh><<46>> (,)

	[content::fc]

	<local-line::fl><<46>>

	<global-line::fg><<44>>

	(.....)

	The final output raw binary has a header of 8 item(s) .

	~ EndianFormat

	~ StringCodec

	~ ChunkSize

	~ StartBoundary

	~ HeaderAddress

	~ ContentAddress

	~ LocalLineAddress

	~ GlobalLineAddress
